Here you will find all the documentation related to the **Device Management Kit (DMK)** for mobile platforms (Android & iOS), written in Kotlin Multiplatform.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  The current version of this Device Management Kit is in **alpha stage** and is
  subject to significant changes.

  Any feedback is welcome — get in touch with us on the [Ledger Discord
  server](https://developers.ledger.com/discord/) to share your thoughts.
</Callout>

## Glossary

- **DMK**: Device Management Kit
- **LDMK**: Ledger Device Management Kit
- **KMP**: Kotlin Multiplatform
